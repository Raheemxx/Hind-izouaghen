About the Artist

Hind Izouaghen is a painter based in Agadir, Morocco. Coming from a deeply artistic family, she developed a strong sensitivity to expression and emotion from an early age. Entirely self-taught, her work blends academic precision with creative freedom.

Artistic Vision

Her work revolves around women empowerment, Amazigh identity, and facial expression. She portrays women as strong and introspective figures, capturing depth and silent narratives.

Style & Influence

Inspired by classical Italian masters like Leonardo da Vinci, her work focuses on anatomy, proportion, and emotional realism, combined with modern storytelling.
