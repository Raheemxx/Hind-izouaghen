About the Artist

Hind Izouaghen is a 31-year-old painter based in Agadir, Morocco. Coming from a family deeply rooted in the arts, creativity has always been part of her environment — her father is a music teacher, her sister a recognized pianist, and her brother a guitarist and bassist.

Artistic Vision

• Women empowerment
• Amazigh identity
• Facial expression and detail
